# Hello World!

Welcome to my developer blog! I'm excited to share my journey as a developer and the things I learn along the way.

## What to Expect

In this blog, I'll be covering topics like:

- **Web Development**: Frontend and backend technologies, frameworks, and best practices
- **DevOps**: Cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, and automation
- **Developer Experience**: Tools, workflows, and productivity tips

## Why Start a Blog?

Writing helps me solidify my understanding of complex topics. By explaining concepts in my own words, I can identify gaps in my knowledge and fill them.

### Learning in Public

I believe in learning in public. Sharing my journey, including mistakes and successes, helps others who might be on a similar path.

## Tech Stack

This blog is built with:

- **Nuxt** - The Vue.js framework for SSR and SSG
- **Nuxt Content** - Markdown-based CMS
- **Tailwind CSS** - Utility-first CSS framework

## Conclusion

Stay tuned for more posts! I'll be sharing tutorials, project walkthroughs, and thoughts on the latest in web development.
